test

$\mathrm{test} = \lambda l.\ \lambda m.\ \lambda n.\ l\ m\ n$

$\mathrm{test}\ b\ v\ w$ は、$b$ が $\mathrm{tru}$ のとき $v$ へ簡約され、$\mathrm{fls}$ のとき $w$ へ簡約される

and

$\mathrm{and} = \lambda b.\ \lambda c.\ b\ c\ fls$

true [a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)nd f[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)lseの例

\[\begin{eqn[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)rr[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)y} \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)nd\ tru\ fls} &=& (\l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) b.\ \l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) c.\ b\ c\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls})\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{tru\ fls}\\ &\to& (\l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) c.\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{tru}\ c\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls})\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls}\\ &\to& \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{tru\ fls\ fls}\\ &=& (\l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) t.\ \l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) f.\ t)\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls\ fls}\\ &\to& (\l[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)mbd[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html) f.\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls})\ \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls}\\ &\to& \m[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)thrm{fls} \end{eqn[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)rr[a](../../algorithms-and-data-structure/07a-array.html)y}\]

値呼び出し戦略によって、内部の抽象よりも $c$ が先に簡約される

or

$\mathrm{tru}$ は前側、$\mathrm{fls}$ は後側の値を返すので、それを利用していい感じに組んでいけばいい

$\mathrm{or} = \lambda b.\ \lambda c.\ b\ \mathrm{tru}\ c\ \mathrm{tru\ fls}$
